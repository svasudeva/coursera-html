About The Author:

Sheree Vasudeva is an author, poet, and social entrepreneur blogger based in San Mateo, California. Rebellious, thinker, moralist who uses her writing to represent moral dilemmas in day-to-day living especially for a dreamer who aspires to be the best and do the best for others. Sheree believes in gender equality and aspires to make a judgement free living for all. Read more at shereevasudeva.com and follow her on Twitter, Instagram Sheree is a proud mother of a lovely daughter and loves to spend quality time with her. She has excessive love for movies and drama; and abides by the saying: "Life is a play; act in it well."
